Recurring Aces NYC Events

Individual Event hosts in our group run a variety of events. How each event is run is determined by the people taking charge and creating the event to share. Soem of them have turned into years long recurring offerings that our members can reliably expect to be on our calendar. Those include events such as:

  • Trans and Gender nonconforming Aces / Aros
  • BIPOC Aces / Aros Discussion
  • NYC Aces Knit/Crochet/Craft a long
  • Aces Book Club Meeting
  • NYC Aces Knit/Crochet/Craft a long
  • Online Ace/Aro Writer's Group
  • Small Group Dinners
  • Hangouts at different bars or public gathering spaces

Check here for all the events for asexual and aromantic people in NYC

Our meetup group has the most up to date events in person, the facebook group Asexuality NYC is hopefully in a close second, while our twitter AcesNYC (or tweeter) is still working on being on time and up to date.
